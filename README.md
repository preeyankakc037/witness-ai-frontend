# 🌿 Witness AI — Your Emotional Companion

**Witness AI** is a premium, minimalist mobile application designed to help users track their mental state, reflect on their day, and gain instant insights into their emotional well-being. Built with React Native and Expo, it provides a calm, inviting space for personal growth and mindfulness.

![Witness AI Banner](https://img.shields.io/badge/Witness--AI-Emotional--Intelligence-4CAF90?style=for-the-badge&logoScale=1.2)

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🌈 Dynamic Home Experience
- **Personalized Greetings**: "Hey, Beautiful" or "Hey, Handsome" – the app welcomes you based on your gender and time of day.
- **Character Stickers**: A cute, gender-aware character (with blinking eyes!) keeps you company on your journey.
- **Day Counter**: Tracks exactly how many days you've been "witnessing" your growth since the moment you joined.

### 📊 Emotion Level Tracking
- **Pill-Shaped Metrics**: Track your **Energy**, **Pressure**, and **Connection** with a sleek, interactive level-tracking system.
- **Instant Insight**: Receive real-time, smart advice based on your current levels. Whether you're in a "high-flow state" or need a "5-minute breather," Witness AI knows exactly what to say.

### 📝 Multi-Step Onboarding
- **Smart Setup**: A 3-step personality wizard designed to understand who you are and what your goals are (Reducing Stress, Clarity, Reflection Habit).
- **Goal-Oriented**: Your experience is tailored to the specific mental health goals you set during sign-up.

### 🤖 Talk to Mindy (AI Chat)
- **Deep Reflection**: Engage in meaningful conversations with **Mindy**, your AI emotional assistant, to explore your feelings and find clarity.

### 🔔 Functional Notifications
- **Stay Engaged**: An interactive notification system that keeps you updated on your goals and daily check-ins.

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

Witness AI is built on the principle of **"Calm Tech"**.
- **Minimalist Aesthetic**: Clean white backgrounds with soft pastel accents (Mint, Peach, Lavender).
- **Premium Micro-Animations**: Subtle "blinking" eyes and smooth transitions to make the interface feel alive.
- **Sophisticated Typography**: Uses high-end serif typefaces for a literary, thoughtful reading experience.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- Node.js (v18+)
- Expo Go app on your physical device (or an iOS/Android emulator)

### Installation
1. Clone the repository:
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/preeyankakc037/witness-ai-frontend.git
   cd witness-ai-v2
   ```

2. Install dependencies:
   ```bash
   npm install
   ```

3. Start the development server:
   ```bash
   npx expo start
   ```

4. Scan the QR code with your phone or press `i` (iOS) or `a` (Android) to launch.
